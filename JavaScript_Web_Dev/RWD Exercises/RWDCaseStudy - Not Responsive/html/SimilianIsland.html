Popular Destinations/Similan Island



Similan Islands is an island chain of nine small islands.It has some Malay roots.It has a national park which is marked as a heritage site by many organisations like UNESCO.Usually Thailand is giving as a different picture.This islands are the most beautiful part of Thailand with beautiful marine life underwater.The blue sea always keep your eye cool. On dipping into this you feel weightless. This is not a very explored area. So it has not yet lost its natural beauty. It offers white sand beaches. The Similan islands are the best diving spot in Thailand.
